About Team Capital Investment Ltd.

Est 2023

Our Story

Team Capital Investment Ltd was established by a group of forward-thinking professionals who recognized the immense potential of Africa’s small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and the largely untapped informal sector. Despite being the backbone of the continent’s economy, these businesses often face systemic challenges, including limited access to capital, inefficient operational structures, and a lack of strategic support.

Guided by a deep commitment to reshaping Africa’s entrepreneurial landscape,Tasie Raphael (Managing Partner/Founder) and Oparinde Babatunde (Managing Partner/Co-Founder) co-founded Team Capital as more than just a venture capital firm—it is a movement to revolutionize funding and business support in Africa. They envisioned an investment firm that not only injects capital into promising businesses but also actively nurtures their growth through strategic mentorship, operational guidance, and ecosystem development.

Strategic Focus

Agriculture

Strengthening food security and agribusiness value chains.

Africa’s agricultural sector is central to food security, employment, and economic sustainability. We invest in agribusinesses, food processing, and supply chain innovations that enhance productivity, reduce post-harvest losses, and improve market access for smallholder farmers. By supporting agritech solutions, sustainable farming practices, and agro-processing, we contribute to a more resilient and efficient agricultural ecosystem.

Construction & Real Estate

Supporting infrastructural development and urbanization.

Rapid urbanization and population growth demand sustainable infrastructure and housing solutions. Our investments in construction and real estate focus on companies that deliver affordable housing, commercial property development, and smart infrastructure projects. We aim to bridge the housing deficit, promote modern construction technologies, and create lasting value in Africa’s built environment.

Financial Technology (Fintech)

Accelerating financial inclusion and digital transformation.

Access to finance remains a major barrier for individuals and businesses across Africa. Our investments in fintech drive financial inclusion, digital payments, and access to credit for SMEs and underserved communities. We support disruptive financial solutions, including mobile banking, blockchain applications, peer-to-peer lending, and alternative financing models, that make financial services more inclusive, secure, and scalable.

Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG)

Enabling businesses to scale production and distribution

The FMCG sector plays a critical role in meeting consumer needs, driving commerce, and fostering industrialization. We invest in manufacturing, distribution networks, and innovative retail models that enhance product accessibility, create employment, and strengthen local supply chains. By supporting businesses that provide affordable, high-quality consumer goods, we empower communities and drive economic participation.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What stages of businesses do you invest in?

We primarily invest in established companies with proven traction, revenue validation, and scalability potential. Our focus is on businesses ready to scale and expand their operations.

How much funding do you provide?

Our ticket sizes typically range from $3500 to X, depending on the business’s needs, growth stage, and sector. We offer flexible financing models, including equity investments, convertible debt, and revenue-based financing.

Can startups apply for funding?

While we primarily focus on established businesses, we occasionally invest in high-potential startups with innovative solutions and strong growth prospects. Startups are encouraged to reach out if they align with our investment criteria.

Do you work with international investors?

Yes, we collaborate with institutional investors, high-net-worth individuals, and family offices globally. If you’re interested in partnering with us, please reach out through our Contact Us page.

How do you select businesses to invest in?

We look for businesses that: Operate in our focus sectors (agriculture, construction/real estate, fintech, FMCG). Demonstrate revenue validation and scalability potential. Have a strong founding team with a clear vision and execution capability. Align with our mission of driving systemic change and economic transformation.

What makes Team Capital different from other venture capital firms?

Our competitive advantage lies in: Sector-Specific Expertise: Deep knowledge of agriculture, construction, fintech, and FMCG. Hands-On Approach: Providing mentorship and operational support. Focus on SMEs and the Informal Sector: Bridging the funding gap for underserved businesses. Commitment to Systemic Change: Addressing structural barriers to business growth in Africa.
